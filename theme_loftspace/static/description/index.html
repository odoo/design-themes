Html5 Responsive Bootstrap Theme for Odoo CMS

Loftspace is a professional responsive HTML5 theme, built using Bootstrap3, the most popular front-end framework for developing responsive, mobile first projects on the web. Just drag & drop the new building blocks you need to easily build your layout. All layouts are made of sections, that you can easily combine to fit your specific project.

Loftspace Customization Tool

Theme Loftspace comes with customization tools. You can easily change the background, the colors and the font combination of your website or you a preset.

Theme Loftspace comes with a lot of options

  • WIDE OR BOXED LAYOUT
  • 6 COLOR SCHEMES
  • 8 BACKGROUND SHADES OR USE YOUR OWN
  • 6 FONT COMBINATIONS
  • 10 BACKGROUND PATTERNS

All the background, color and font combinations were made to work together.  You can not go wrong with the settings.

Minimalistic Flat Design

Loftspace was made with easy customization and branding in mind. Based on simple, modern and typographical elements, it allows you to easily customize your website.

6 Prebuild Presets

You don't have the time to play with options? Don't worry, Loftspace gives you six full presets wich combine colors, fonts and background.

This theme has an additional eCommerce plugin.
